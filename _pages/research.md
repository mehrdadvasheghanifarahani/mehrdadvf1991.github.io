## Research Interests

My research focuses on the fundamental and applied aspects of flow and transport in porous media, including:

- Two-phase flow and wettability effects  
- Multiphase transport in micro- and nano-confined systems  
- Lattice Boltzmann method development  
- Hydrogen generation and storage  
- Drying, salt precipitation, and reactive transport  
- High-resolution pore-scale imaging (microfluidics, X-ray imaging)
